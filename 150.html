The average price tag of these ecosystem services are estimated to be US $ 33 trillion annually.
•
This is nearly twice the value of the global gross national product GNP (US $ 18 trillion).
•
Out of the total cost of various ecosystem services:
➢
Soil formation accounts for about 50 per cent
➢
Recreation for about 10 percent
➢
Nutrient cycling about 10 per cent.
➢
The cost of climate regulation and habitat for wildlife are about 6 per cent each
