Water pollution and its control
With the increase in pollution in water, the government has passed Water (Prevention and Control of Pollution) Act, 1974
to safeguard our water resources.
Domestic sewage and industrial effluents
0.1% percent impurities make domestic sewage unfit for human use. Solids are easy to remove, but dissolved salts such
as nitrates, phosphates and other nutrients are difficult to remove. Domestic sewage primarily contains biodegradable
organic matter which can be decomposed using microorganisms.
