To produce large quantities of recombinant protein, large vessels known as
bioreactors
are used. A bioreactor provides
the optimal conditions for achieving the desired product by providing optimum growth conditions (temperature, pH,
substrate, salts, vitamins, oxygen).
Basic parts of a bioreactor:
1.
Agitator
2.
Oxygen Control system
3.
Foam control system
4.
Temperature control
5.
pH control
6.
Sampling port
7.
Inlet
8.
Outlet
