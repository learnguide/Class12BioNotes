Microbes as biocontrol agents
Biocontrol is defined as use of biological methods to control plant diseases and pests. Conventionally, pesticides and
insecticides are being used for the control of diseases and pests. But these chemicals are extremely toxic and harmful.
Biological control of pests and diseases.
