4. Aquaculture/Pisciculture
Management of catching, processing as well as selling fishes such as Rohu, Catla, and Hilsa etc. is known as pisciculture.
Due to more requirement of fishes’ different methods are employed to promote pisciculture. Aquaculture is defined as
growing of aquatic animals and selling them for commercial purpose. There are two types of aquaculture-
extensive
aquaculture
and
intensive aquaculture
. Extensive aquaculture is done in ocean, natural and man-made lakes, rivers etc.
Intensive aquaculture exists in ponds and tanks.
