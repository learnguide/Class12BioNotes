Biotechnology applications in medicine
Biotechnology has contributed a lot in medicine industry. The use of biotechnology in medicine is known as medicinal
biotechnology. They provide methods for the formation of genetically modified insulin known as
humulin
. This helps in
treatment of large number of diabetes patients. Earlier insulin was extracted from the pancreas of the slaughtered animals
such as cattle and pigs. Such insulin has chances to cause allergies to humans. The most important issue with the
production of insulin is formation of mature and assembled insulin.
