Microbes as biofertilizers
The excess use of chemicals and their harmful effect has forced the farmers to switch to organic farming. Organic
farming use biofertilizers. Biofertilizers are organisms that enrich the nutrient quality of the soil. Biofertilizers contain
bacteria, fungi and cyanobacteria.
Rhizobium
bacteria is found in leguminous plants such as pea, bean, etc. This
bacterium helps in absorption of nitrogen required by the plants. Other bacteria can also fix nitrogen such as
Azospirillum
and
Azotobacter.
