Microbes in Human Welfare
Microbes are beneficial as well as detrimental to the welfare of human beings. Microbes are utilized in many ways for
human beings as explained below-
Microbes in vaccination and antibiotics/microbes in industrial products
Microbes are used to synthesize different products that are useful for humans. Beverages and antibiotics are the most
common product obtained from microbes. For large scale production and use of microbes in industries, special vessels
are used, known as fermenters.
