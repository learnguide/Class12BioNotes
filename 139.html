Mutualism
is an interaction where both the species interacting will benefit each other. Lichens and mycorrhiza
are most important example of mutualism. Lichens is mutualistic relationship between algae and fungi.
Mycorrhiza is relation between the fungi and the roots of the higher plants.
•
Amensalism
is a relationship in which one species is harmed and other interacting species is neither harmed nor
benefitted.
